What is Curiio?

CURIIO promotes a positive discourse between parents and children about sexuality, adolescence and reproductive health. Harnessing the power of internet, age-based videos and articles on above mentioned matters would be made available through our platform. Under parental guidance, the performance of the child can be tracked and monitored. It also deals with the perplexities of a parent raising a young child in today’s world and the ‘uncomfortable’ conversations that comes along with it. For parents, the platform provides various blogs and articles emphasizing on the need of sex education.

Why Curiio?

Sexuality education has been contentious since forever but the urgent need of reliable information is unquestioned. CURIIO provides a safe space for open discussions and trustworthy information. In India, such discussions are censored and deemed as inappropriate. This defiance led to the withdrawal of ‘adolescent education programme’, introduced in 2006-07. The reluctance towards such conversations pushes young people to seek falsified sources leading to misinformation and misguided behaviour. Thus, it is required from parents to initiate such conversations and make children aware about their bodies and its functionalities.
